Frequently Ask Questions

Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions to help you understand our services better, and if you need more help, feel free to contact us!

Should I stop letting my dog sleep with me at night?

It’s fine if it doesn’t disturb your sleep or health. Otherwise, giving them their own bed is better.

Is it okay to dress up your dog?

Yes, as long as your dog is comfortable and not restricted. Clothes can help in cold weather or for protection.

Why do dogs like belly rubs so much?

Dogs enjoy belly rubs because it feels good and shows trust. It also helps release happy hormones.

Is a warm dry nose a sign of illness in dogs?

Not always, as a dog’s nose can naturally change throughout the day. But if there are other symptoms, it’s best to check with a vet.
